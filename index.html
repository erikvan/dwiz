GC essentials

There’s plenty more to the Gold Coast than the glittering skyline of Surfers Paradise. Open your eyes and hearts to the likes of Burleigh Heads, Palm Beach, Nobby Beach and Currumbin. We’ve fallen in love with the GC over the years and we want you too on your little getaway.

Things to do

The Night Quarter

This place is a little bit of a drive but it’s worth visiting as the happening night market is filled with every type of cuisine, unique shops and live music. This place is special to us because it’s where we first saw the band that is now playing at our wedding! Open Friday & Saturday 4-10pm.

Check it out

1 Town Centre Drive, Helensvale

Things to do

Miami Marketta

Another happening night market in Miami specialising in street food, cocktails, dessert and live music – what more do you want from your life? Try the beef wagyu gnocchi - boy you won’t be disappointed!

Check it out

23 Hillcrest Parade, Miami

Things to do

Skypoint Observation Deck & Climb

For those wanting some adventure, this is for you! At 270m in the air, this is Australia’s highest external building climb. You get 360 degrees views of the Gold Coast and beyond so we highly recommend you put this on your bucket list. Even if you are afraid of heights, you can’t make any excuses because Carly completed this (although petrified) and if she can do it, anyone can!

Check it out

9 Hamilton Ave, Surfers Paradise

Things to do

Pacific Fair

If you want to know where the best place is to go shopping in the GC this is it! You can shop until you drop at this outside shopping centre that has it all.

Check it out

Hooker Blvd, Broadbeach

Things to do

Currumbin Rock Pools

Want to get away from the hustle and bustle of the Gold Coast beaches? These rock pools are for you! A natural swimming pool with rope swings, sheltered picnic tables, and toilet facilities – you can chill out here all day. If you are game enough (or as Carly says stupid enough) to jump off the rock face like Darren does you might feel a little scared, even though the local kids make it look so easy!

Check it out

Currumbin Creek Rd, Currumbin Valley

Things to do

Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary

This is the place to see some of Australia’s most famous animals. One of Carly’s favourite activities is where the lorikeets eat from your hand!

Check it out

28 Tomewin St, Currumbin

Places to stay

Broadbeach Eats

There’s a bunch of spots to satisfy your bellies in beautiful Broadbeach – we recommend the youngins head here for a lunch but venture out for dinner on the Saturday night. Our favourites include Mario’s Italian, Main Street Burger Bar, Gyoza and The Loose Moose. And make sure you end off in the right way, with 2 scoops at the best Gelato in town.

Places to eat

Hideaway Kitchen & Bar

A stone’s throw away from those staying at the Meriton. Don’t be fooled by its outward appearance, it really is a hideaway! It serves up tasty Asian street food and the wait staff are super friendly. It is a super popular spot so if there is a group of you we suggest you book in advance.

Check it out

2657 Gold Coast Hwy, Broadbeach

Places to eat

Etsu Izakaya

This place is worth the 5 minute Uber from the Meriton. It is another little hideaway on the Gold Coast Highway so keep an out of for the lit up red lantern which means they are open. We promise you this is as good as Japanese food gets, with some even saying this is their favourite restaurant in the whole of Australia. We always leave Etsu saying ‘That was meccha oishii!’ (a term Darren used too much in Japan which means ‘very delicious’). It’s another restaurant you should book in advance because it is super popular!

Check it out

2440 Gold Coast Hwy, Mermaid Beach

Places to eat

The Collective

A bit of a twist on the standard restaurant, this place has five restaurants in one with a bar and a rooftop bar. You can sit outside under the sun where you face the challenge of trying to decide which restaurant to choose! The Poke, Green Super salad and lemon, lime and bitters were really yummy here! It’s a must. Get there.

Check it out

1128 Gold Coast Hwy, Palm Beach

Places to eat

The Star Casino, Garden Kitchen and Bar

For those staying at the Meriton, walk across the highway and there you will find the Star Casino. The real gem of this place is the Garden Kitchen and Bar. Sit outside in the sun and treat yourself to a jug of Pimms, our favourite drink! You will never want to leave the GC once you find out about this place as this is what life is about… sorry in advance!

Check it out

Casino Level, The Star Gold Coast, Broadbeach

Places to eat
